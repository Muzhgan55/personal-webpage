Muzhgan Rahimi

Hello! My name is Muzhgan, and I was born on March 9, 2007, in Badakhshan province. I was studying at the Afghan-Turk Girls School in Kabul, where I reached 9th grade before the government collapse disrupted my studies. Although I could not continue in a formal classroom, I have never stopped learning. I am a curious learner who finds joy in exploring new ideas whether through coding, reading books, solving math problems, or writing stories. I believe in the power of knowledge, creativity, and perseverance. Music inspires me deeply, and I often turn to it as a source of comfort and motivation. My journey has been shaped by challenges, but I see them as opportunities to grow. Every day, I strive to keep learning, building, and creating step by step toward my dreams.
